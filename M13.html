BETA-013

" Funny how you aren't running in absolute fear from me. Most workers usually wouldn't even get this close to me ahaha... "

-BETA-013 after being introduced to Syna

BETA-013, later named Mei, is a character from the currently concepted Murder Drones: KILL C0DE. She is a kind and cuirous Murder Drone who wants to instead make peace with the Worker Drones after being "kicked out" her former squad of Murder Drones for being too soft and useless design.

Appearance

013 has features similar to that of a typical female Disassembly Drone with neon yellow eyes and medium silverish hair pulled into a low and loose ponytail in the back. Like other Murder Drones, she has a black headband with five sections of lights and a long black tail ending in a large syringe. Unlike her headband, her syringe is filled with a slightly transparent silver liquid istead of neon yellow liquid.
